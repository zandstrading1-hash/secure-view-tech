About us

A decade of protecting metro Detroit.

Secureview Tech has spent over ten years installing security and smart-home systems people rely on — with a five-star record built one clean install at a time.

Secureview Tech started with a simple idea: security work should be done properly, explained plainly, and stand behind its name. Over a decade later, that is still how every job runs — whether it is a single doorbell camera or a full commercial surveillance and network build.

We are a full security and smart-home integrator, which is a fancy way of saying one team wires your whole property and makes it all work together. Cameras, alarms, access control, lighting, audio, and the network behind them — designed together, installed together, supported together.
