Privacy

Your privacy — and your footage.

Most privacy pages only cover the website. Ours also answers the question every camera customer actually has: who can see my footage?

Your footage stays yours

This is the part that matters most, so we will say it plainly: the footage your cameras record belongs to you. Secureview Tech installs and, when you ask us to, services your system — but we are not watching your cameras, and we do not keep copies of your recordings.

  • We do not access your live cameras or stored footage unless you specifically ask us to — for example, to troubleshoot a problem you have reported.
  • When we do need access for service, it is with your permission, for that task, and nothing is copied or retained afterward. [Confirm this matches how you actually operate]
  • Your recordings are stored on the equipment at your property and/or the cloud service you choose — under your account and your control.

Information this website collects

When you contact us through this site, we collect what you send us — your name, phone, email, and details about your property — for the sole purpose of responding to your request and providing service.

How we use it

  • To reply to your estimate request and schedule work.
  • To provide support and service on systems we install.
  • We do not sell your information to anyone.
