Accessibility

Built to work for everyone.

We want every visitor — including people using screen readers, keyboards, or magnification — to be able to reach us and understand what we do.

Our commitment

Secureview Tech is committed to making this website accessible and usable for people of all abilities. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

What we have built in

  • Clear color contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • Full keyboard navigation, with a visible focus outline on every link and control.
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images.
  • Text that resizes cleanly and a layout that reflows down to small screens.
  • Reduced-motion support for visitors who prefer less animation.

Report a problem

If any part of this site is hard to use, we want to fix it. Email secureview.tech@gmail.com or call (586) 230-0772 and tell us what you ran into — including the page and what happened — and we will respond and work to resolve it.

Ongoing. Accessibility is not a one-time checkbox. As we add real photos and features, we will keep this standard in place.
